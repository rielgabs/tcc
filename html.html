Program Offering — Bachelor of Science in Information Technology

College Vision

The College of Information Technology of Tagoloan Community College Envisions to become one of Region 10’s leading IT school that produces the best, dynamic, competent, compassionate, committed men and women proficient in the use of state-of-the-art technology.

College Mission

The College of Information Technology is committed to the core values espoused by the College, particularly to promote quality and excellence an IT education by:

  • Adherence to the commission on higher education
  • Spearheading initiatives to response to the fast-changing technology
  • Collaborating the community extension programs for planning and development

BSIT Curriculum

The BS Information Technology (BSIT) program includes the study of the utilization of both hardware and software technologies involving planning, installing, customizing, operating, managing and administering, and maintaining information technology infrastructure that provides computing solutions to address the needs of an organization. The program prepares graduates to address various user needs involving the selection, development, application, integration, and management of computing technologies within an organization. CHED Memorandum Order No. 25 s.2015
